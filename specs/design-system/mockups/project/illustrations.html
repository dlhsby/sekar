A pinwheel
that never
stops.

SEKAR is Javanese for flower, and Bahasa for the system that watches over Surabaya's green spaces — Sistem Evaluasi Kinerja Satgas RTH. The mark is a pinwheel of 8 kinetic petals — one per rayon (Timur 1/2, Barat 1/2, Utara, Selatan, Pusat, Taman Aktif) — spinning around a yellow DLH Surabaya center. The mark moves because the system never stops moving: shifts rolling, GPS updating, syncs flushing. This sheet locks the logo, app icon, splash, empty-state illustrations, onboarding scenes, and field iconography. All vector, all token-driven, all neo-brutalism 2.0.

01

The mark

Primary lockup, geometry, color variants, and the don'ts. The mark works mono down to 24 px and full-color above 40 px. Wordmark uses Space Grotesk 800, never substituted.

Etimologi

Sekar (Jw.) — bunga. Eight petals = eight rayons: Timur 1, Timur 2, Barat 1, Barat 2, Utara, Selatan, Pusat, Taman Aktif. The yellow center = DLH Surabaya, the institution coordinating all rayons. The kinetic spin says the system never stops: shift demi shift, sinkronisasi tanpa henti.
