# Welcome to SEKAR 🌿

**SEKAR** (Sistem Evaluasi Kinerja Satgas RTH) is a worker performance management application for the Surabaya Department of Environment (DLH). The system is designed specifically to monitor and manage field work with real-time GPS technology, photo verification, and digital work reports.

## What is SEKAR?

<p align="center"><img src="/img/illustrations/onb-monitor.svg" alt="Monitoring illustration" width="240" /></p>

SEKAR helps field teams and supervisors to:

- **Digital Attendance Recording**: Clock in/out with photo verification and GPS
- **Real-time Tracking**: Monitor worker position directly on a map
- **Work Reports**: Document activities with photos and videos
- **Task Management**: Create and track tasks from supervisors to workers
- **Analytics & Reporting**: Dashboard for rayon heads and management
- **Plant Management & Pruning**: Plant catalog, maintenance, and pruning requests

## Who Uses SEKAR?

The SEKAR system is designed for various roles:

### For Field Workers
- **Satgas (Satuan Tugas)** — Field workers who perform daily activities
- **Linmas (Lembaga Masyarakat)** — Security/environmental workers
- **Korlap (Koordinator Lapangan)** — Coordinator who leads and supervises teams

### For Management & Supervisors
- **Kepala Rayon** — Monitor rayon performance and create reports
- **Admin Data** — Manage user and area data
- **Admin Sistem** — Technical application management
- **Top Management** — View analytics and overall reports
- **Superadmin** — Full access to all features

### For External Stakeholders
- **Staff Kecamatan** — Submit tree pruning requests
